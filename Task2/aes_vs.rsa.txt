AES (Advanced Encryption Standard)
Type: Symmetric encryption (same key to lock and unlock)

Speed: Very fast

Use: Good for encrypting large files

Security: Very strong, used by governments

RSA (Rivest-Shamir-Adleman)
Type: Asymmetric encryption (different keys to lock and unlock)

Speed: Slow

Use: Good for sharing secret keys

Security: Strong, but needs long keys
